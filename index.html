Manga

Manga are Japanese graphic novels or comics, typically read from right to left. They combine expressive artwork with serialized storytelling and span a wide range of genres.

Manhua & Manhwa

Manhua refers to Chinese comics, known for their vibrant art and themes rooted in Chinese culture, fantasy, and martial arts.

Manhwa refers to Korean comics, with a strong presence in digital platforms (like webtoon).
